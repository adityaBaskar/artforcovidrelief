Art for COVID Relief

The Art for Covid Relief is a charitable initiative. We are a group of volunteer artists who are happy to deliver artwork to you, when you donate for a cause.

Who are we?
The Art for Covid Relief aims to encourage the act of donation towards NGOs working on covid relief, by bringing people the positive incentive of art. The initiative aims to nurture NGOs working towards covid relief while also celebrating art and the exchange of it. We are a vibrant, growing community of over 35 volunteering artists. We work on a wide range of custom art orders such as creative portraits as gifts, conceptual art and even interior decor pieces.
What is this fundraiser about?
